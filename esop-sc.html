Steering Committee

The current composition of the Steering Committee is
  • Amal Ahmed (PC Chair 2018, permanent member since 2020)
  • Luis Caires (PC Chair 2019, SC Chair since 2022)
  • Peter Müller (PC Chair 2020)
  • Brigitte Pientka (Permament member since 2017)
  • Ilya Sergey (PC Chair 2022)
  • Nobuko Yoshida (PC Chair 2021)
The Steering Committee of ESOP was created in 1998, when the ETAPS cluster was created with ESOP as one of the key conference. Before that ESOP was a biannual conference and was looked after by Reinhard Wilhelm, one of the founding fathers of ESOP. Since its creation the ESOP Steering Committee was chaired by:
  • Hanne Riis Nielson (1998-2007)
  • Christopher Hankin (2007-2011)
  • Giuseppe Castagna (2011-2017)
  • Peter Thiemann (2017-2022)
  • Luis Caires (2022-present)

Past members

  • Peter Thiemann (SC Chair 2017-2022)
  • Jan Vitek (Permanent member 2014-2020)
  • Philippa Gardner (Permament member since 2017-2019)
  • Giuseppe Castagna (SC Chair 2011-2017)
  • Philip Wadler (Permanent member 2011-2017)
